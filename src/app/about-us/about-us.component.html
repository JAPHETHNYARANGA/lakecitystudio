When the band plays together and things just click, or the artist finds the perfect mood – our studios have been designed to capture these moments. Whether it’s your first time in a studio or if the control room is like a second home, Lake City Recording Studio unique comfort and character will make you feel at home in the studio. WHY RECORD WITH LAKE CITY RECORDS?

We all want the same thing: quality, professional sounding recordings that bring an added dimension to our live performances and epitomise our creative talents. And that is where we come in. We have created a number of spaces to serve different purposes, for songwriting or recording, and capturing the essence of a live performance. Whatever stage you’re at with your musicianship, Lake City Recording Studio can, and will, save you time and money. More importantly, it will foster and focus your creativity like no other recording space. With our engineers you will get a professional, fresh and unbiased opinion of how your performance is sounding – and the result will sound good because it does, not because you desperately want it to. We understand that home recordings have their place, however Lake City Recording Studio will help you get results faster, better and in a more creative environment.
